For coaches

Built around how you actually recruit

Greenlight Recruit consolidates junior golf data into a single, coach-friendly view—so you can spend less time hunting for information and more time evaluating fit.

How it works

We aggregate and normalize data from leading junior golf ranking sources, then layer on performance context, academic snapshots, and coach tools like shortlists and notes.

  • Unified profiles with AJGA, Junior Golf Scoreboard, and WAGR snapshots
  • Scoring averages, course rating/slope, and field strength for key events
  • Ranking trend badges to highlight who’s rising, stable, or falling
  • Private notes and tags visible only to your staff
Why it’s better than manual search

Instead of juggling spreadsheets and browser tabs, you get a single recruiting cockpit that mirrors how you talk about players in staff meetings.

  • Replace scattered notes with structured shortlists by class and roster need
  • Export staff-ready PDFs and CSVs in seconds
  • See performance trends instead of one-off scores
  • Keep your board updated as rankings and results evolve
Compliance-aware positioning

Greenlight is designed to respect recruiting rules and communication windows. You control how and when you contact prospects; we focus on surfacing the right information at the right time.

  • View-only profiles until contact windows open
  • Internal notes and tags stay within your staff
  • No public “rankings” of your board—everything is private to your program
Access & pricing

Start with a trial, then choose the plan that fits your program’s workflow—from a single coach seat to a full staff license.

  • Single-coach access for head or assistant coaches
  • Program licenses with multiple seats and shared shortlists
  • Custom onboarding for conferences or multi-program groups
For juniors & parents

Clarity on how your data appears

Greenlight explains how ranking and performance data is presented to coaches—and gives you a clear path to keep your profile accurate and compelling.

How your ranking data appears

We surface your existing rankings and results from leading junior golf sources, then present them in a way that helps coaches understand your body of work.

  • AJGA, Junior Golf Scoreboard, and WAGR snapshots
  • Key finishes in strong-field events
  • Scoring averages over time, not just one hot week
Strengthening your profile

Beyond rankings, coaches want to see who you are as a student-athlete. Greenlight highlights the pieces that matter most.

  • Academic snapshot: GPA, test scores, and intended major
  • Swing and on-course video clips
  • Short bio and notes on schedule, travel, and goals
Privacy & control

You can request updates or changes to your profile information so that coaches see the most accurate version of your game and academics.

  • Request corrections to basic info and academics
  • Ask to add or remove video links
  • Options to limit visibility in certain cases
