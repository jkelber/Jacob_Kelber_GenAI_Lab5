About the Project

Greenlight Recruit was created to solve a simple but persistent problem in college golf recruiting: coaches spend far too much time jumping between AJGA, Junior Golf Scoreboard, WAGR, spreadsheets, emails, and scattered notes. The information exists — but it’s fragmented, inconsistent, and slow to work with during the busiest parts of the recruiting cycle.

Our mission: Give college golf coaches a single, unified recruiting cockpit that consolidates rankings, performance data, and evaluation tools into one clean, modern interface.

Project Goals

  • Unify AJGA, Junior Golf Scoreboard, and WAGR data into a single, coach-friendly view.
  • Provide meaningful context behind scores — field strength, scoring trends, and event quality.
  • Give coaches tools they actually use: shortlists, notes, tags, and exportable reports.
  • Reduce time spent searching for information so coaches can focus on evaluating fit.
  • Offer juniors and parents clarity on how their data appears and how to keep it accurate.

Why This Matters

College golf recruiting is increasingly competitive, and coaches are expected to evaluate more players across more regions than ever before. Greenlight Recruit helps programs stay organized, informed, and efficient — without adding complexity or noise.

Who This Is For

  • College coaches who want a faster, clearer way to evaluate junior golfers.
  • Junior golfers & parents who want transparency and accuracy in how their data is presented.
  • Programs looking to modernize their recruiting workflow.

This project is still evolving, and new features will continue to be added as coaches provide feedback and the junior golf landscape changes.
